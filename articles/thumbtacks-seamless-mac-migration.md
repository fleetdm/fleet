### The Challenge

Thumbtack first encountered challenges with their current Mac MDM through their support channels. Thumbtack had to rely on ticket-based systems to raise support issues, and feedback was slow. 


### The Search for a Solution

Thumbtack evaluated numerous Mac MDM vendors.  Most vendors were too small and some did not offer mature support for CI/CD operations. Thumbtack wanted a vendor with some track record and also with to ensure that they would not be required to seek another vendor in the near future.

<blockquote purpose="quote">
I really like … how robust \[Fleet’s\] API is” - Adam Anklewicz
</blockquote>

After a comprehensive review of options available in the market, Thumbtack selected Fleet. Its extensive support for Apple MDM configurations, it’s strong customer referrals and its robust Infrastructure-as-code support gave Thumbtack the confidence that Fleet was the right option.

 
### Why Fleet?

Fleet offered a robust set of controls for managing Mac devices. Fleet’s API controls allowed Thumbtack to access Apple MDM configs easily and to replace existing controls with their current MDM.

In addition, Fleet’s community engagement with the MacAdmins community helped provide Thumbtack confidence that other enterprise customers had great experiences with the company and software.

<blockquote purpose="quote">
All of Fleet’s issues being public GitHub is huge because I can just search to see if anyone else is having the same problem - Adam Anklewicz
</blockquote>


### The Results

The migration to Fleet went very smoothly with over 90% of devices migrated without any IT intervention. In addition, the team now enjoys a strong support relationship with dedicated Slack channels and fast response times.

Fleet’s open-source model turned out to be even more valuable than Thumbtack initially expected. Their IT team could dive into the documentation, identify issues, and even troubleshoot software behavior directly by reviewing the source code. This level of transparency was refreshing and gave them far greater confidence in Fleet.

The shift to GitOps has also been a major win for Thumbtack’s team. They can now inspect and approve changes before deployment, enabling tighter collaboration and delivering much stronger confidence in how their endpoint environment is managed.
