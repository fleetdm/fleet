# Industrial DevOps company automates endpoint management with Fleet

An industrial DevOps company provides version control and collaboration tools for automation and PLC programming. Its environment includes macOS, Windows, and cloud-based workstations used for demos and development.

Fleet helps the company manage both persistent devices and ephemeral environments with greater consistency.

## At a glance

* **Industry:** Industrial DevOps and automation  
* **Devices managed:** ~85 user devices plus cloud workstations  
* **Primary requirements:** Advanced configuration, GitOps workflows, osquery visibility  
* **Previous challenge:** Limited configuration control and poor visibility

## The challenge

Before Fleet, the company relied on Rippling, which did not support advanced configuration or custom deployments.

Ephemeral cloud workstations were especially difficult to manage. The team needed a way to ensure these short-lived systems were secure and configured correctly during critical use cases like customer demos.

## The evaluation criteria

The team focused on two capabilities:

1. **Advanced endpoint configuration**  
    Move beyond basic, pre-built management workflows.  
2. **osquery integration**  
    Enable real-time visibility across all systems.

## The solution

Fleet gave the team a platform that supports both persistent and ephemeral devices.

The company uses scripting and GitOps workflows to manage configuration across its fleet. Fleet also enables real-time validation of device state, which is critical for ensuring demo environments are always ready.

The open-source model aligns with the company’s engineering culture and allows teams to troubleshoot issues directly.

## The results

Fleet improved both visibility and operational efficiency.

* **Better control over cloud workstations:** Ephemeral environments are now easier to manage.  
* **Faster issue resolution:** Teams can identify and fix problems quickly.  
* **Reduced tool overlap:** One platform replaces multiple management workflows.

## Why they recommend Fleet

For this company, the biggest benefit is control and flexibility. Fleet enables the team to manage complex environments through automation, visibility, and a unified approach.
