
# European financial services company improves compliance visibility with Fleet

A European financial services company provides payment infrastructure for businesses of all sizes. As a regulated institution, it must meet strict audit and compliance requirements across its device fleet.

Fleet helps the company manage Linux systems more effectively while improving real-time compliance visibility across its environment.

## At a glance

* **Industry:** Financial services  
* **Devices managed:** Mixed macOS and Linux environment  
* **Primary requirements:** GitOps workflows, real-time visibility, on-premise hosting  
* **Previous challenge:** Slow compliance data and limited Linux visibility

## The challenge

Before Fleet, the company relied on legacy tools that provided limited visibility and slow updates.

Compliance data was often delayed due to once-daily inventory cycles. This made it difficult to respond quickly to security issues or demonstrate compliance during audits. Linux systems were also harder to manage consistently, which created gaps across engineering workstations.

The team needed faster access to device data and a more reliable way to enforce compliance.

## The evaluation criteria

The team focused on three priorities:

1. **GitOps workflows**  
    Use version-controlled configuration for auditability.  
2. **Real-time osquery queries**  
    Run live queries instead of waiting for scheduled updates.  
3. **On-premise hosting**  
    Maintain full control over data for regulatory compliance.

## The solution

Fleet gave the team a platform that supports both real-time visibility and code-driven workflows.

The company built automated compliance systems that integrate device data into version-controlled processes. Fleet also supports custom automation across macOS and Linux, including package management and compliance baselines that update dynamically.

The open-source model helps technical users inspect and debug issues directly, which improves confidence in the platform.

## The results

Fleet improved both compliance speed and operational flexibility.

* **Faster compliance response:** Teams can detect and address issues in minutes instead of waiting for daily updates.  
* **Better Linux coverage:** Linux systems are now managed more consistently.  
* **Improved audit readiness:** Version-controlled workflows make it easier to demonstrate compliance.

## Why they recommend Fleet

For this company, the biggest benefit is GitOps for compliance.

Fleet helps the team meet regulatory requirements with real-time data and code-based workflows that are easier to audit and maintain.

## About Fleet

Fleet is the single endpoint management platform for macOS, iOS, Android, Windows, Linux, ChromeOS, and cloud infrastructure. Trusted by over 1,300 organizations, Fleet empowers IT and security teams to accelerate productivity, build verifiable trust, and optimize costs.

By bringing infrastructure-as-code (IaC) practices to device management, Fleet ensures endpoints remain secure and operational, freeing engineering teams to focus on strategic initiatives.

Fleet offers total deployment flexibility: on-premises, air-gapped, container-native (Docker and Kubernetes), or cloud-agnostic (AWS, Azure, GCP, DigitalOcean). Organizations can also choose fully managed SaaS via Fleet Cloud, ensuring complete control over data residency and legal jurisdiction.
