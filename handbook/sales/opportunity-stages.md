# Opportunity stages

The following details each stage in our new sale process and what is needed to move a deal through each stage, including: 
- Stage name
- Probability: The current likelyhood of this deal going "Closed-won".
- Definition: The high-level, 1-2 sentence definition of where this deal is at, written in present tense.
- Timeline: The time-based thresholds we've put on this stage before it needs to be reviewed and possibley closed.
- Exit criteria: What's required to take place before moving this opportunity to the next stage.


## Meeting set

> If a renewal oppty is confirmed that it will churn, we set the stage to "Meeting set" (0% probability) to signify the churn.

***Probability***: 0%

***Definition***: A qualified ICP prospect has scheduled a first meeting with Fleet *OR* had a meaningful conversation with a Fleet Salesperson and wants to learn more.

***Timeline***: 60 days
- 30 Days from oppty creation to deliver the meeting. 
- 30 Days from meeting date to move the opportunity up or out.

***Exit criteria***:
- The prospect account has been qualified as being ICP.
- Meeting date and time have been confirmed by the prospect *OR* a meaningful conversation has already taken place.
- Pre-discovery has been completed (not BANT), but we understand what they want to "see" or "discuss".
- ARR is greater than $25k.
- A meaningful "Next step", with a due date, has been identified and recorded on the oppty.


## Discovery

***Probability***: 10%

***Definition***: We've confirmed the prospect meeting, we're actively identifing thier pain, and working to confirm that Fleet is the best possible solution.

***Timeline***: TODO

***Exit criteria***:
- The prospect meeting was delivered *OR* a meaningful conversation has already taken place.
- Discovery completed (not just a demo) including:
  - A clearly defined usecase documented in SFDC.
  - Initial budget range discussed.
  - Mutual agreement that Fleet is worth further evaluation  
- Approximate Timeline understood.
- The AE has identified KPIs/SLAs/desired outcomes/key business metrics that would signify success for the individual prospect and thier organization. 
- The SC has delivered Fleet's “gold standard" product demo and understands the prospect's pain enough to qualify/disqualify thier usecase as a technical fit for Fleet's current or very near-term feature set.
- ARR ≥ $75K
- A meaningful "Next step", with a due date, has been identified and recorded on the oppty.


## Scope

***Probability***: 20%

***Definition***: We've confirmed that Fleet can solve the prospect's pain, and we're identifing what needs to be tested and verified for Fleet to obtain the "technical win".

***Timeline***: TODO

***Exit criteria***:
- Workshop completed (Advanced, tailored product demo)  
- Technical requirements identified and documented with a clear next step toward PoV.
- Success criteria verified, as defined by:
  - Success criteria is written down in a Google doc, email, etc. and recorded in SFDC.
  - We have documented, written proof (in the form of an email) that the propspect has agreed to the proposed success criteria and timeline.
- Economic Buyer identified in SFDC.
- A meaningful "Next step", with a due date, has been identified and recorded on the oppty.



## EB (economic buyer)

***Probability***: 30%

***Definition***: Fleet and the prospect have agreed on a set of criteria and timeline for testing, an we're confirming executive-level support for the project.

***Timeline***: TODO

***Exit criteria***:
- The AE has spoken with EB *OR* strong champion confirms EB support for the project.
- Funding source understood
- Decision process understood
- Project confirmed as viable  
- Agreement to proceed to PoV, including:
  - PoV scope re-confirmed with EB or strong champion.
  - Start and end dates defined.
  - Weekly checkpoints scheduled.  


## PoV (proof of value)

> When renewals are created, they should be set to "PoV (Proof of Value)".

***Probability***: 50%

***Definition***: Fleet is being evaluated against agreed success criteria.

***Timeline***: TODO

***Exit criteria***:
- Champion actively engaged
- Fleet meets agreed requirements
- A meaningful "Next step", with a due date, has been identified and recorded on the oppty.


## Justification

***Probability***: 70%

***Definition***: Technical win achieved. Business case presented.

***Timeline***: TODO

***Exit criteria***:  
- Business value summarized and executive readout delivered.
- Economic Buyer agrees to initiate commercial discussion/procurement process.
- Legal/procurement engaged, sales conversation complete.
- A meaningful "Next step", with a due date, has been identified and recorded on the oppty.


## Negotiation (final)

***Probability***: 90%

***Definition***: Commercial terms are being finalized.

***Timeline***: TODO

***Exit criteria***:
- Pricing approved  
- Target signature date defined
- Contract out for signature  
- No material open blockers
- A meaningful "Next step", with a due date, has been identified and recorded on the oppty untill the oppty is closed.
- Fleet has received a fully executed order form signed by the prospect complete with PO (if required).
