## Review boosted social media performance

We review boosted social media performance to understand what works and improve future campaigns.

### What we review

* Click-through rates (CTR) for boosted posts  
* Click-through rates for awareness and ABM campaigns  
* Which ads perform well and should be reused or expanded, and add them to the evergreen (Awareness) campaign 

### How to review

1. Open current boosted campaigns in Target experiments.  
2. Review CTR across all ads.  
3. Compare performance between awareness and ABM campaigns.  
4. Identify top-performing ads.  
5. Add strong performers into awareness and ABM rotations.

### Benchmarks

* Boosted ads typically see low CTR, around **0.39%–0.65%**.  
* Organic posts often perform better, usually **1%–5%+**, depending on audience familiarity and engagement.

Use these ranges to evaluate performance. Focus on relative improvement, not just absolute numbers. Targeting experiences that are performing well, boosting them for 5 business days to increase engagement.

### Ongoing monitoring

* Check ABM campaigns every few days to confirm they are running correctly after updates or restarts.  
* Review campaigns daily to catch issues early and maintain performance.
