# Clay

## What is Clay?

Clay is a data enrichment and lead generation platform used at Fleet for finding, enriching, and verifying contact and company data. It helps sales and marketing teams build accurate prospect lists, enrich existing data, and identify new opportunities.

## Systems it integrates with

Clay integrates with the following systems:

- **Salesforce** - Enriched data and new leads can be synced to Salesforce accounts and contacts
- **LinkedIn** - Can pull data from LinkedIn profiles
- **Email verification services** - Validates email addresses
- **Company data providers** - Enriches company information from various data sources
- **Zapier** - Can be used to automate data flows between Clay and other tools

## How to get access

1. **Sales and Marketing team members** who need data enrichment capabilities can request Clay access.

2. **To request access**, [create an issue](https://github.com/fleetdm/confidential/issues/new?assignees=&labels=%3Ahelp-it-and-enablement&projects=&template=1-custom-request.md&title=) with IT & Enablement. Include:
   - Your role and why you need Clay access
   - Approval from your manager or department head
   - Specific use case (e.g., lead enrichment, prospecting, data verification)

3. **Account provisioning** is handled by IT & Enablement. Once approved, you'll receive login credentials via 1Password.
