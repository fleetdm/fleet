# LinkedIn Sales Navigator

## What is LinkedIn Sales Navigator?

LinkedIn Sales Navigator is LinkedIn's premium sales tool used at Fleet for finding, connecting with, and engaging prospects on LinkedIn. It provides advanced search filters, lead recommendations, InMail messaging capabilities, and account insights to help sales teams build relationships and close deals.

## Systems it integrates with

LinkedIn Sales Navigator integrates with the following systems:

- **Salesforce** - Contact and account data can be synced between LinkedIn Sales Navigator and Salesforce
- **Dripify** - Works alongside Dripify for automated LinkedIn outreach
- **Gong.io** - Can provide context for sales conversations
- **LinkedIn** - Native integration with the main LinkedIn platform

## How to get access

1. **Sales team members** typically receive LinkedIn Sales Navigator access on their first day as part of onboarding, especially for go-to-market (GTM) roles.

2. **For other team members** who need access, [create an issue](https://github.com/fleetdm/confidential/issues/new?assignees=&labels=%3Ahelp-it&projects=&template=1-custom-request.md&title=) with IT. Include:
   - Your role and why you need Sales Navigator access
   - Approval from your manager or department head
   - Your LinkedIn profile URL

3. **License provisioning** is handled by IT. Once approved, you'll receive login credentials via 1Password.
