# Figma

## What is Figma?

Figma is a collaborative design tool used at Fleet for UI/UX design, prototyping, and design system management. It enables the Product Design team and engineers to collaborate on design work, create design specifications, and maintain design consistency across Fleet's products and marketing materials.

## Systems it integrates with

Figma integrates with the following systems:

- **GitHub** - Design files and specifications can be linked to GitHub issues and pull requests
- **Slack** - Design updates and comments can be shared in Slack channels
- **Webflow** - Design assets can be exported for use in website development
- **Various design tools** - Supports plugins and integrations with other design and development tools

## How to get access

1. **Product Design team members** typically receive Figma access as part of their onboarding.

2. **For other team members** who need access (e.g., Engineers, Marketing), [create an issue](https://github.com/fleetdm/confidential/issues/new?assignees=&labels=%3Ahelp-it-and-enablement&projects=&template=1-custom-request.md&title=) with IT & Enablement. Include:
   - Your role and why you need Figma access
   - Approval from your manager or the Product Design team

3. **License provisioning** is handled by IT & Enablement. Once approved, you'll receive an invitation to join Fleet's Figma organization.
