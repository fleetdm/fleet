# DocuSign

## What is DocuSign?

DocuSign is an electronic signature platform used at Fleet for sending, signing, and managing contracts, NDAs, order forms, and other legal documents. It streamlines the document signing process and ensures secure, legally binding signatures.

## Systems it integrates with

DocuSign integrates with the following systems:

- **Salesforce** - Contract and order form management is tracked in Salesforce opportunities
- **Google Workspace** - Document templates and storage
- **Email** - Document delivery and signature notifications

## How to get access

1. **Sales team members** typically receive DocuSign access as part of their onboarding.

2. **For other team members** who need access, [create an issue](https://github.com/fleetdm/confidential/issues/new?assignees=&labels=%3Ahelp-it-and-enablement&projects=&template=1-custom-request.md&title=) with IT & Enablement. Include:
   - Your role and why you need DocuSign access
   - Approval from your manager or department head

3. **Account provisioning** is handled by IT & Enablement. Once approved, you'll receive login credentials via 1Password.
