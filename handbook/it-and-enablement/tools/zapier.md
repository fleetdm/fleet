# Zapier

## What is Zapier?

Zapier is an automation platform used at Fleet to connect different applications and automate workflows without writing code. It enables various integrations between Fleet's tools, such as syncing data between systems, automating notifications, and streamlining repetitive tasks.

## Systems it integrates with

Zapier connects many of Fleet's tools and systems:

- **Salesforce** - Various automation workflows for CRM data
- **Slack** - Automated notifications and message routing
- **GitHub** - Issue and pull request automation
- **Google Workspace** - Calendar, Sheets, and Gmail integrations
- **Email systems** - Automated email workflows
- **Various other tools** - Custom integrations as needed

## How to get access

1. **Zapier access is typically managed centrally** by IT & Enablement rather than individual user accounts.

2. **To request a new automation workflow**, [create an issue](https://github.com/fleetdm/confidential/issues/new?assignees=&labels=%3Ahelp-it-and-enablement&projects=&template=1-custom-request.md&title=) with IT & Enablement. Include:
   - What systems you want to connect
   - What the automation should do
   - Approval from your manager or department head

3. **IT & Enablement will review and implement** the automation workflow if approved.
