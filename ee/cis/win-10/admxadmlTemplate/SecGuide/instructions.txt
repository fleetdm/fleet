This section contains settings for configuring additional settings from the MS Security
Guide.

This Group Policy section is provided by the Group Policy template SecGuide.admx/adml
that is available from Microsoft at this link (https://techcommunity.microsoft.com/t5/microsoft-security-baselines/security-baseline-final-for-windows-10-v1903-and-windows-server/ba-p/701084)

This template must be downloaded and applied to set the group policies and run checks against them.
